Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the VEXARO website.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide basic information about how users interact with the site.

2. How Cookies May Be Used

Cookies may be used to ensure website functionality, improve user experience, understand how visitors interact with the site and maintain basic technical performance.

3. Types of Cookies

Essential cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Analytics cookies

These cookies may be used to collect anonymized data about how the website is used in order to improve performance and user experience.

4. Third-Party Services

The website may use third-party services such as analytics tools or embedded content providers. These services may use their own cookies according to their respective privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the website.

6. Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
